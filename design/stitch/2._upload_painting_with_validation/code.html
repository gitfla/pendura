Curatorial Notes

  • 01
    Planar Alignment

    Ensure the painting is shot head-on. Trapozoidal distortion can affect the digital placement precision.

  • 02
    Neutral Exposure

    Avoid heavy filters. A clean, balanced exposure allows our renderer to apply environmental shadows accurately.

  • 03
    Edge Definition

    If the painting has raw edges, photograph it against a high-contrast background for better edge detection.
